FAQ

Common questions

Which brokers do you support? +
We currently support Zerodha (Kite API), AngelOne (Smart API), Upstox, and Fyers. If you use a different broker, reach out and we'll check compatibility.
Do I need any coding knowledge? +
Zero. You just need to explain your strategy clearly — in plain English, a PDF, or a whiteboard sketch. We handle all the technical implementation.
How long does it take to build my algo? +
Typical turnaround is 5–10 business days depending on strategy complexity. You'll receive a backtest report before we go live, and you have full approval rights.
Is my money safe? Do you have access to my funds? +
Absolutely not. The algorithm connects to your broker account via API keys that have order-placement permissions only — no withdrawal access. Your capital is always under your control.
Can I request changes to the algo after it's live? +
Minor logic tweaks (e.g. adjusting SL/target levels, changing position size) are included in the monthly fee. Major strategy overhauls may incur a small additional charge, discussed upfront.
What if I want to stop the monthly service? +
Cancel anytime with 7 days' notice. The algo code belongs to you — we'll hand it over so you can self-host if you wish.
