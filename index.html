FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need coding knowledge? +
None at all. You share your trading logic in plain language — entry conditions, exit rules, risk settings — and we handle all the MQL5 coding from scratch.
Which MT5 brokers are supported? +
Any broker that offers MetaTrader 5 is supported — Exness, XM, IC Markets, FP Markets, Pepperstone, OctaFX, HFM, FXTM, and hundreds more. If they have MT5, we can run on it.
How long does it take to deliver? +
Most algos are delivered within 5–7 business days. Complex strategies with multiple conditions or multi-symbol logic may take up to 10 days. We always confirm timelines upfront.
What if the algo doesn't perform? +
We build exactly what you specify and include a backtest report. Market conditions change — we offer parameter adjustment support for 30 days. We don't guarantee profit, but we guarantee the logic is correct.
Can I get the source code? +
Yes. The full MQL5 source code is yours upon payment. You own the algo completely and can modify it, share it, or use it on multiple accounts as you wish.
Can you replicate a manual strategy? +
Absolutely. If you have a discretionary strategy with clear, rule-based triggers, we can automate it. Schedule a call and walk us through your setup — we'll assess feasibility for free.
